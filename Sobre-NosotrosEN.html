Who are we?

We are team Barceló FC from the SMR1 vocational training course at the ALMI Study Center in Bilbao. We are participating in Challenge 5 RobotALMI, a project where we have designed, assembled, and programmed real robots to compete at a European level. In a record time of 3 weeks and with a team of three people, we have integrated hardware, code, and security.

Our Robot Fleet

01. Line-Follower Robot

Arduino UNO

An autonomous robot that detects black lines on a white background using infrared sensors. Its logic is based on the constant adjustment of servomotors to maintain the trajectory.

What is our purpose?

Our goal is to demonstrate our technical problem-solving capacity in a high-pressure environment. In just 3 weeks, we have achieved what seemed impossible: assembling, programming, and deploying a functional fleet of robots controlled both autonomously and via remote systems.

This project represents the culmination of our learning in SMR1, uniting networks, programming, and physical assembly into a single technical success.
